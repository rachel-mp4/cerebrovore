we're so back

full thoughts on everything here

gist is that it's taken me some time to get to a point where i can program a site like this. not fully sure where it's going. if you want involved, let me know! i can teach you any sort of thing you want to know if i know basically how to do it, or we can work together towards it if i don't

consider this just the earliest alpha. probably i won't make any big structural shifts that ruin everything here,

and I WILL NEVER JUST RANDOMLY TAKE THE SITE DOWN!,

but it's entirely possible in the near future i'll have to archive and read-only the state of the alpha site as we move into beta or full-release

right now, please DON'T share this site widely. if you have a close friend or two from OG brainworm or whatever, it's OK to share it with them, but DO NOT LINK THIS ON THE OPEN INTERNET

ESPECIALLY NOT IN SUS SPACES

(that includes the website formerly known as brainworm. i'll have a chat with op worm (hi op worm if you're reading this now))

my roadmap right now is that alpha = no widespread sharing, because anyone can create an account

in beta, i'll have tied account creation to invite codes. this way, we'll be able to see who invited whom, and if there's any bad actors producing lots of invite codes to raid the site etc... we'll have the capacity to figure out the relationships there and stop that

in full-release, right now i think that account creation will be allowed for people who don't have invite codes for a one time payment. this is the model that Something Awful used, but besides that, instead of thinking of this as an "imageboard," i've been conceptualizing it more as a "digital coffee house." in a coffee house, the purchasing of coffee etc... is more a sort of contract that gives you some buy-in to participate in the social space, rather than a payment for some concrete goods. that's just how i've been thinking about it at least

nothing will enter memory, everything was on the lines, between the lines, in the AND that made one and the other imperceptible, without disjunction or conjunction but only a line of flight forever in the process of being drawn, toward a new acceptance, the opposite of renunciation or resignation—a new happiness?

i'm excited to see wherever this ends up

-rachel
